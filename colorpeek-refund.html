ColorPeek Refund Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

We want you to be satisfied with your ColorPeek Pro purchase. This policy explains when and how refunds are issued.

1. Refund Window

Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of purchase.

2. Eligible Reasons

Refunds are issued for the following reasons:

  • Technical issues — The app has a bug or malfunction that prevents normal use, and our support team cannot resolve it within 5 business days.
  • Feature not as described — A core feature advertised on our product page is missing or materially different from what was described.
  • Billing errors — Duplicate charges, incorrect amounts, or unauthorized transactions.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • More than 14 days have passed since the purchase date.
  • The request does not fall within any of the eligible reasons above.
  • The license key has been shared, resold, or activated on more than 3 Macs in a pattern that suggests abuse.
  • The purchase was made with a stolen payment method or fraudulent credentials.
  • Issues caused by external factors outside our control (macOS updates, third-party software conflicts, hardware issues).

4. How to Request a Refund

Send an email to support@wayknow.tech with:

  • Your order number (e.g., ORD-XXXXXXXXXXXXX)
  • Your license key
  • The reason for your refund request
  • For technical issues: a description of the problem and any relevant screenshots

We will review your request and respond within 2 business days.

5. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed through our payment provider (Creem) and credited back to your original payment method. Processing time depends on your bank or card issuer, typically 5–10 business days.

6. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank or card issuer without first contacting us, we reserve the right to revoke the associated license key(s) and terminate access to Pro features.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The version in effect at the time of your purchase applies to that purchase.

8. Contact

For refund inquiries, contact support@wayknow.tech.
