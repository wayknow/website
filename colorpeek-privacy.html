Privacy Policy

ColorPeek — Last updated: July 3, 2026

Our Privacy Promise

ColorPeek is a 100% local macOS application. Everything happens on your Mac. We don't have servers to collect your data even if we wanted to.

What We Collect

Nothing. The free version of ColorPeek makes zero network requests. No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting, no usage statistics. The color history and palettes are stored exclusively in macOS UserDefaults on your device.

What the Pro License Verification Sends

If you purchase a Pro license, ColorPeek sends only your license key and a device identifier to api.wayknow.tech to verify your license status. No color data, no usage data, no personal information is ever transmitted. The license check is cached locally for 7 days.

Data Storage

All color history, palettes, and preferences are stored locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults. You can clear all ColorPeek data by deleting the app and its preferences file.

Third-Party Services

ColorPeek does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only network request the app makes is Pro license verification to api.wayknow.tech.

Children's Privacy

ColorPeek does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update this page. But our core promise — zero data collection — will never change.
