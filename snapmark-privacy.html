Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2025

SnapMark ("the extension", "we", "us", or "our") respects and protects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. Please read it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 What We DO NOT Collect

SnapMark is designed with 100% local processing at its core. Here's what we do not collect:

  • Personal information: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, IP address, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Screenshot content: All screenshots and annotations are processed entirely within your local browser. They never leave your device.
  • Browsing history: We do not read, record, or track which websites you visit.
  • Location data: We do not collect your geographic location.
  • Usage analytics: We do not collect any usage data, feature usage frequency, or any form of analytics — not even anonymous statistics.

1.2 Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's chrome.storage.local API to store the following data on your device only:

  • User preferences: Your settings (theme selection, shortcut configuration, etc.).
  • Screenshot history: Screenshot records you save within the extension (stored in your browser's local storage; never uploaded).
  • License information: If you purchase the Pro version, license verification data is stored locally.

All of the above data is stored exclusively on your device. You can delete it at any time by clearing your browser data.

2. How We Use Data

Since we collect no data, there is no data usage to describe. All functionality (capture, annotation, export) executes entirely within your browser and requires no network connectivity beyond the initial extension installation.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with any third party — because we collect no data in the first place.

4. Data Security

Since all data resides on your local device, data security is ensured by your device and Chrome browser's security mechanisms. We recommend:

  • Keeping Chrome updated to the latest version
  • Using OS-level security features (screen lock, disk encryption)
  • Not installing browser extensions on untrusted devices

5. Third-Party Services

SnapMark does not integrate any third-party services. No analytics tools (such as Google Analytics), no advertising platforms, no crash reporting tools. If you purchase the Pro version through Creem, the payment process is handled by Creem — please refer to Creem's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

6. Permission Justification

SnapMark requests the following Chrome permissions, each with a specific purpose:

  • activeTab: Access the current tab only when you actively click the extension icon, for capturing screenshots.
  • scripting: Inject screenshot and annotation scripts into the current page (only upon your action).
  • storage: Store your preferences and screenshot history locally.
  • clipboardWrite: Copy screenshots to your clipboard (only upon your action).

We do not request tabs, cookies, history, webRequest, or any other permissions.

7. Children's Privacy

SnapMark does not collect data from anyone, and therefore does not collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date. Since we do not collect your contact information, we cannot proactively notify you of changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
