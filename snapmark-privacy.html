SnapMark Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

SnapMark is a Chrome extension for screenshot capture and annotation. This policy explains what data SnapMark handles and how.

Data Collection

SnapMark does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on any remote server. All processing — screenshot capture, image annotation, file export — happens entirely inside your browser, on your device.

What SnapMark Accesses

  • Screenshot data: Captured via Chrome's captureVisibleTab API and stored temporarily in Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local) on your device. This data is never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.
  • Active tab URL: Read only to determine whether the current page is a restricted page (e.g. chrome://). The URL is not stored, logged, or transmitted.
  • License key: If you enter a Pro license key, it is stored locally in chrome.storage.local and validated locally. No server-side validation is performed.

Permissions

SnapMark requests the following Chrome permissions, and uses them only as described:

  • activeTab: To capture screenshots of the current tab only when you click the extension icon.
  • scripting: To inject minimal scripts for full-page scrolling, element selection, and delayed capture. Scripts are injected only when you initiate a capture, and are removed immediately after.
  • storage: To save your settings, screenshot history, and license key locally on your device.
  • clipboardWrite: To copy annotated screenshots to your clipboard when you choose to.

Third-Party Services

SnapMark does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. No data is shared with any third party.

If you purchase the Pro version through Creem, the payment process is handled by Creem — please refer to Creem's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

Your Control

You can clear all SnapMark data at any time by:

  • Opening Chrome DevTools on the extension's service worker and clearing storage, or
  • Removing the extension from Chrome

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
