Why CookieClear Exists

A story of abandonment — and the safe, trustworthy alternative

EditThisCookie — with 3M+ users and 11,000+ reviews — was removed from the Chrome Web Store in July 2024. The developer had abandoned it years earlier. A malicious copycat quickly filled the vacuum, tricking 50,000+ users into installing an extension with obfuscated code that steals Facebook credentials. There is no safe, maintained, trustworthy cookie editor for Chrome.

The remaining alternatives are either abandoned (Cookie-Editor — no updates since 2024), subscription-only (CookieJar — $4.99/mo), or show intrusive ads. CookieClear is the safe, modern, open-source replacement — and it's completely free.
