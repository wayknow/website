SnapMark Refund Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

We want you to be satisfied with your SnapMark Pro purchase. This policy explains our refund process.

1. Refund Eligibility

You may request a refund within 14 days of your purchase if:

  • The license key has not been activated on more than 2 devices
  • The refund request is submitted from the same email address used for the purchase

2. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • More than 14 days have passed since the purchase date
  • The license key has been shared, resold, or activated on multiple devices in a pattern that suggests abuse
  • The purchase was made with a stolen payment method or fraudulent credentials (as determined by our payment provider)

3. How to Request a Refund

Send an email to support@wayknow.tech with:

  • Your order number (e.g., ORD-XXXXXXXXXXXXX)
  • Your license key
  • Reason for the refund request (optional but helpful)

We will review your request and respond within 2 business days.

4. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed through our payment provider (Creem) and credited back to your original payment method. Processing time depends on your bank or card issuer, typically 5-10 business days.

5. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank or card issuer without first contacting us, we reserve the right to revoke the associated license key(s) and terminate access to Pro features.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The version in effect at the time of your purchase applies to that purchase.

7. Contact

For refund inquiries, contact support@wayknow.tech.
